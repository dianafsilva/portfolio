ABOUT

I am a 26 year old girl with an interest for art in all its forms, with a special focus on photo editing, video editing and illustration. I have a minor degree in Sound and Image from ESAD in Caldas da Rainha and I am currently doing a masters in Design and Multimedia from FCTUC in Coimbra. I have some work experience working as a freelancer but I would like to expand my knowledge and do more works. I have always been a creative person, demonstrating it in all my work and projects. I am looking for new opportunities for learning and experience in the area, in order to acquire new skills and become a better artist and professional, which is my main goal.
